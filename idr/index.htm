Imagine a fine art reproduction as perfect as the original.

Ideal Digital Reproduction uses the highest quality materials and equipment available. We use a satellite 3D digital scanning system that takes original artwork and reproduces it with total accuracy and maximum resolution. The image reproduced is vibrant and incredibly detailed. A professional technician will then work with you to crop, adjust, highlight and color manage your image. At IDR, we're artists too. We'll work with you from start to finish, through image capture, proofing, printing, curation, and shipping.

 

With Ideal Digital Reproduction, life imitates art.

Our Roland high-fi jet ink printer uses a computerized application of up to 8 unique colors on a variety of mediums including canvas, hi-gloss, matte papers, fine watercolor papers, and proofing film. With smoother blends and rich colors, the Roland printer dramatically increases the realism, accuracy, and intensity of color in printmaking.

Our printer is the first wide format ink jet to achieve true high-resolution printing, creating incredible realism and vibrancy. The hi-fi jet uses a special formulated pigmented ink that creates true-to-life color and unrivaled accuracy through the use of 64-nozzle micro-piezo print heads, the most reliable and precise print heads in the world.
