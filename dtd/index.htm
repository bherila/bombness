What is I.D.R.?

Ideal Digital Reproduction, (I.D.R.) offers to discriminating fine art, photography and advertising clients worldwide, a digital reproduction process that brings original art to another commercial level.

What is the giclee process?

The term “giclee” was coined in 1990 to describe fine art created by using a digital print technology, it has come to describe a new era in original collaborative and reproductive digital printmaking.

Giclee is French for “spray”. That is what our state-of-the-art Roland printers do. The print heads spray ink; up to 1440 drops of ink on a square inch. The end result is a breath-taking, and true to the spirit reproduction of the artists’ original vision.

How does Ideal Digital Reproduction’s giclee process work?

The professionals at I.D.R. use a ZBE satellite 3-D digital scanning system that takes original artwork and reproduces it with total accuracy and maximum resolution. Alternative transparencies maybe scanned into the computers through an EverSmart Scitex scanner. The image reproduced is vibrant and incredibly detailed.

Our I.D.R. experts work with you to crop, adjust, highlight and color manage an image. Painstaking care is brought to bear before the actual prints are reproduced. With Ideal Digital Reproduction, life imitates art.

With 1440 dots per square inch, it is close to impossible to distinguish between the giclee print and the original.

How is the I.D.R. giclee process unique?

Color
Our Roland high-fi jet ink printer use a computerized application of six ink colors (with two other optional colors) on a variety of mediums including canvas, hi-gloss, matte papers, fine watercolor papers, and proofing film. With smoother blends and rich colors, the Roland printer dramatically increases the realism, accuracy, and intensity of color in printmaking. The end result is a razor sharp, proof quality image that is hard to distinguish from the original.

Accuracy
With Barco color correct monitors for color management and 5500º Kelvin light booths for proofing, we are able to provide you excellent color stability in your giclees.

The Roland is the first wide format ink jet to achieve true high-resolution printing creating incredible realism and vibrancy. The hi-fi jet uses a special formulated pigmented ink that creates true to life color and unrivaled accuracy through the use of a 64 nozzle micro-piezo print heads, the most reliable and precise print heads in the world.

You can choose from six print modes ranging from a very fast 360 dots per square inch mode, a finely detailed 720 dots per square inch output, or a photo quality 1440 dots per square inch image reproduction.
